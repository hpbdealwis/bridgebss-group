Purpose

Complex problems don't fix themselves.

Behind every broken system, every process that slows people down, every gap that holds organisations back, there is a solution waiting to be designed. We exist to find it.

We believe that by understanding a problem deeply enough, technology and innovation can fix what is broken and build what is missing. That belief is the thread that runs through everything Bridge does, across talent, technology, and health.

We don't treat symptoms. We solve problems at their root.

About Bridge

The people who
built this.

Bridge is led by a multidisciplinary founding team with deep expertise across innovation, finance, operations, and technology, and decades of global experience building scalable systems and delivering real-world impact.

The founding team

Dr. Kerry Gilmore

Chief Executive Officer

An inventor and research scientist with over two decades spent turning complex problems into patented, commercial solutions. Dr. Gilmore holds six patents and has authored more than 50 peer-reviewed publications. He has co-founded five companies, each built on the same principle: that rigorous science and commercial pragmatism are not opposites. At Bridge, he applies that discipline to workforce quality treating talent placement as a system to be designed, tested, and continuously improved.

Roshan De Silva

Chief Operating Officer

A technology delivery leader with over 20 years of experience driving complex, large-scale implementations across enterprise and government sectors in Sri Lanka, the Maldives, and the Middle East. Most recently Vice President of Smart Building and Smart Factory Solutions at MillenniumIT ESP, where he led large-scale digital transformation programmes end to end. He brings the delivery rigour that makes complex, multi-market engagements actually work.

Sanjiv Malalasekara

Chief Financial Officer

A finance leader with a career built at institutions where precision is non negotiable. Senior roles at Morgan Stanley and Credit Suisse in London, Ernst & Young across Sydney and London, and ANZ as Corporate Banking Head of Strategy across Asia Pacific. Also Managing Director and Partner at Arinma Holdings. He brings institutional-grade financial governance and cross-regional commercial insight to Bridge.

Gayan Hanthige

Chief Technical Officer

A digital transformation specialist with over a decade leading technology implementations across ERP, IoT, and enterprise software systems. At Bridge, he leads the technical layer that underpins how talent is found, assessed, and matched applying end-to-end systems thinking to the workforce problem.

Global presence

Bridge operates across the United States, the United Kingdom, the UAE, and Sri Lanka, with senior teams on the ground in each region, bringing local fluency and long-term commitment to every engagement.
